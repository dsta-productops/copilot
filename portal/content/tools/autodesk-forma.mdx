## What it does

AI-assisted spatial and platform modelling. Used in the engineering domain for environment modelling, platform fly-throughs, and capability visualisation in spatial context.

Where Luma generates from natural-language description and Napkin works at the diagram level, Forma works at the structured-spatial level — useful when the team needs to reason about physical placement, dimensions, and interactions in space.

## When to use it

- Design phase for engineering teams
- Spatial capability development — when physical context, placement, or scale is part of the capability
- Cross-platform integration — when modelling how one capability interacts with others in physical space

## How to access

[autodesk.com/products/forma](https://autodesk.com/products/forma). Cloud-hosted.
