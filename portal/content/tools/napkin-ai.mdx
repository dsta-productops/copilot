## What it does

Text-to-diagram generation. Type a description of a process, a system, or a relationship; Napkin renders it as a clean diagram you can edit and export.

Most diagrams in early-phase work are wrong by the next review anyway — Napkin reduces the cost of drawing them in the first place, which makes teams more willing to revise them.

## When to use it

- Design phase for engineering teams — system diagrams, data flows, capability decomposition
- Anytime a team is reasoning about relationships and the conversation would benefit from a shared visual
- Stakeholder presentations — when a diagram is more durable than slide bullets

## How to access

[napkin.ai](https://napkin.ai). Web-based.

## Output expectations

- Outputs are starting points — most diagrams need one round of human edits before they communicate clearly
