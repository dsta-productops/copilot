## What it does

Generates 3D content and video from natural-language descriptions. In the engineering domain, Luma is used for producing fly-throughs of platforms, immersive scenario visualisations, and motion sequences that help operators engage with a capability concept before any physical prototype exists.

Pairing Luma with an operational scenario produced by the [operational-scenario generator](/prompts/operational-scenario-generator) gives the team a visual-plus-narrative artefact that operators can engage with end-to-end.

## When to use it

- Design phase for engineering teams
- Concept development — to make spatial and temporal aspects of a capability tangible
- Operator engagement sessions — where a flat image isn't enough to convey the experience

## How to access

[lumalabs.ai](https://lumalabs.ai). Cloud-hosted.

## Output expectations

- Treat outputs as conceptual visualisations, not engineering specifications
- Validate dimensions and physics with domain experts before any decision rides on them
